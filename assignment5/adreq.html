MIS Program

Admission Requirements

Robert Morris University’s Graduate School offers many opportunities to add a Master’s Degree to your credentials. With day, evening and weekend class options, the busy professional is offered multiple opportunities to include college in their schedule. Furthermore, MGSM offers multiple scholarship options and international grants to make earning a master’s degree an even smarter investment.

To Enroll:

  • Apply online
  • Submit official transcripts
  • Submit professional resume
  • Apply for Federal Student Loans
  • Admission's checklist
  • International admission's checklist

For those with less than a 3.25 undergraduate GPA, please submit the following additional documentation:

  • Submit a personal statement
  • Submit two letters of recommendation
